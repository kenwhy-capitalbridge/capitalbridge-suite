# Capital Stress Model — User manual

**Capital Stress** explores how a simplified **capital + withdrawal + return band** story behaves under **Monte Carlo–style** uncertainty and optional **stress severity** (e.g. market shock presets). It answers “how fragile is this structure under varying paths?” in principle—not a forecast of your portfolio.

## Before you start

- **Membership** must allow **Capital Stress** (trial plans often **restrict** full stress use; follow header or gate messaging).
- **Currency** may default from your profile (RM, SGD, etc.).

## Typical workflow

1. Set **starting capital**, **withdrawal** (monthly or equivalent), **return range** (lower/upper %), **years**, **confidence** or simulation count (as shown), and **currency**.
2. **Run simulation** when prompted—many panels stay descriptive until a run completes.
3. Read **depletion** / **durability** visuals, **scenario** strips, and **recommended adjustments** as **illustrative** levers—not instructions.

### The Lion’s Verdict (entitlement-dependent)

- **Before a run**: you may see **System Insight** with a note to **run the simulation** first.
- **After a run**:
  - **Paid**: full **Lion’s Verdict** narrative tied to the stress snapshot.
  - **Trial / limited**: **System Insight (limited access)** in the same area—no full verdict copy.

## PDF / print

- **Print or save report** (when available) follows your **lion access** tier: full Lion block **paid**; **trial** may omit or soften Lion narrative in the captured report—see output.

## Saving

- Server persistence is typically **paid**; trial users may be read-only for saves.

## Tips

- Treat **stress presets** as **sensitivity**, not prophecy.
- If headline **fragility** is high, revisit assumptions in **Capital Health** and **Income Engineering** with an adviser.

## Disclaimer

Stress outputs are **scenario-based**. Past or simulated paths do not guarantee future results. Not personal advice.
