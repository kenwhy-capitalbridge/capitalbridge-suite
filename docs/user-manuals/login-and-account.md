# Login & account — User manual

The **login** app handles **authentication**, **pricing**, **checkout**, and routes you back to the **platform** or **model** apps with a valid session.

## Common URLs

- **Sign in / sign up** — typically `/access` (and related recovery links from email).
- **Pricing** — `/pricing` (plans, regional display where implemented).
- **Checkout** — `/checkout` or query-driven flows from pricing cards.

## Creating access

- Follow **sign up** or invitation flow; complete **email verification** if required.
- Choose or confirm **advisory market** / region where asked—this often drives **currency** shown in calculators.

## Pricing and plans

- Plans may include **trial** (time-limited), **monthly**, **quarterly**, and **strategic** (longer / flagship) access.
- **Displayed prices** can vary by **market** (MY, SG, US, etc.)—see the pricing page for your session’s region.
- **Payment** runs through the checkout your organisation configures (e.g. Billplz); complete payment in the same browser when instructed.

## Return to a model app

- Pricing links may include a **`model=`** parameter so **BACK** or post-checkout navigation returns you to the correct app **dashboard**.

## Password and security

- Use **forgot password** / recovery from the login screen.
- Do not share OTP or magic links; Capital Bridge staff will not ask for your password.

## GITEX / coupons

- Event flows (e.g. **GITEX**) may use a **dedicated page** or **coupon redeem** path documented in your event materials. After redeeming, follow the link to **platform** or the guided model experience.

## Problems signing in

- Clear cookies for `thecapitalbridge.com` only if advised by support.
- Ensure you use the **same email** you registered with.
- Corporate VPNs or blockers can interfere with auth redirects—try another network if redirects loop.
