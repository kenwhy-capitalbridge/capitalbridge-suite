# Capital Bridge Platform — User manual

The **platform** app is your signed-in **home** for the advisory framework: where you land after login, see **module** status, open model apps, and (depending on tier) access **strategic** or execution-oriented content.

## Access

- Visiting the platform **without a session** usually redirects to the **login** app (`/access`) with a **return URL**.
- After sign-in, you should see **dashboard** or **framework** content appropriate to your **membership**.

## What you can do here

- **Open model apps** — Forever Income, Income Engineering, Capital Health, Capital Stress (according to entitlement).
- **Profile / settings** — Often includes **advisory market** (drives display currency in model apps), **name**, and related fields—exact screens vary by release.
- **Framework journey** — Visual or copy-driven **stages** (e.g. modules 1–4) may show **locked** vs **available** based on plan (**trial** vs **strategic**, etc.).

## Session and navigation

- When a model app sends you **back** through **session sync**, use that path so your **session** and **active slot** stay consistent (avoids unexpected sign-out).

## GITEX / campaign access

- **Guided** or **event** access types may show **banners** or **redirects** (e.g. toward platform home instead of a full model dashboard). Follow on-screen instructions; upgrading is via **login / pricing**.

## Membership tiers (high level)

- **Trial** — Explore models with **limits** (e.g. no server save, limited Lion / PDF depth).
- **Paid monthly / quarterly / strategic** — Broader access; **strategic** tier unlocks **execution**-oriented framing on the platform where configured.

**Exact feature matrix** is defined by your contract and the live product—this manual stays descriptive.

## Support

- **Billing / login issues** → login app and your account email flows.  
- **Model behaviour** → use each model’s manual and your adviser.
