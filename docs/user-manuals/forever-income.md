# Forever Income Model — User manual

The **Forever Income** app helps you explore whether your **capital stack** (cash, investments, property equity you include) can support your **lifestyle spend** under the **return and inflation assumptions** you set. It is an educational modelling tool—not personal financial advice.

## Before you start

- **Sign in** with a Capital Bridge account that has an **active membership** for this model. If you are sent to pricing, complete checkout on the login site first.
- **Display currency** usually follows your **advisory market** set on your Capital Bridge profile (you change region there, not inside the model).
- Outputs depend entirely on **your inputs**. They are **not** guarantees of future performance.

## Main screen overview

### Lifestyle and contributions

- Choose **Monthly** or **Annual** expense basis and set your **expenses** and **family contribution / offset** (income that reduces net draw from capital).
- Adjust **capital allocation**: cash, investments, real estate to unlock.
- If you use **real estate**, you can set **loan cost** and **time horizon** so the model reflects financing.

### Returns and inflation

- Set **expected return** (nominal, annual) and **inflation** as you wish to stress-test the story.

### Strategic gap analysis

- See **target capital**, **total assets**, **progress**, **runway / horizon**, and a **pie** view of how capital is used.
- Status labels (e.g. growth phase, critical gap) summarise the snapshot under current inputs.

### The Lion’s Verdict (entitlement-dependent)

- **Paid members** see the full **Lion’s Verdict** block: narrative aligned to your snapshot.
- **Trial / limited plans** may see **System Insight (limited access)** with an option to view **pricing** for full access—no decisive score narrative in that mode.

## Saving and restoring

- **Save** and **restore** (and **saved history**) depend on your plan. Trial users are typically **not** entitled to persist snapshots to the server; paid users can use the header controls when available.

## Report (PDF)

- **Save report / generate PDF** is available when the model allows export for your account (often when sustainability conditions are met—see on-screen button state).
- The report is generated through Capital Bridge’s **export pipeline**. **Trial-class** exports may show a **trial snapshot** and **omit** full Lion narrative; **paid** exports include the full verdict section when your tier allows it.

## Tips

- Change **one lever at a time** (e.g. contribution or return) to see how sensitive the story is.
- Use **BACK** in the header to return to the platform hub without losing your session when that flow is shown.

## Disclaimer

This calculator is for **advisory and educational** purposes. Projections use **your** assumptions and do not guarantee future results. Discuss outputs with a **qualified professional** before acting.
