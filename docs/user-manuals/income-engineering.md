# Income Engineering Model — User manual

**Income Engineering** models how **income, expenses, loans, unlockable capital**, and **investment buckets** interact so you can see **coverage**, **stress months**, and sustainability labels under your assumptions. It is educational—not personal advice.

## Before you start

- **Active membership** required; otherwise you will be redirected to **login / pricing**.
- **Currency** matches your **advisory market** on your profile (no separate in-app currency switch in the standard setup).
- **GITEX / guided access**: you may see a banner that full PDF depth or certain features need a standard membership; follow on-screen messaging.

## Main sections

### Expectations setting

- **Expenses** — your lifestyle spend assumptions.
- **Income** — salary and other income lines the model should count.
- **Assets / unlock** — mechanisms you mark as unlocking capital (with loan terms where relevant).
- **Investment buckets** — how you allocate among buckets and expected returns you assign.

### What this means

- Read **net position**, **sustainability status** (e.g. green / amber / red), and **coverage** stories the model derives from your inputs.

### The Lion’s Verdict (entitlement-dependent)

- **Paid**: full **Lion’s Verdict** panel with score-aligned narrative.
- **Trial / limited**: **System Insight (limited access)** teaser and link to **plans**—same position on the page, without full verdict copy.

## Download report (PDF)

- Use **Download report** when shown. The app captures a **print layout** of your scenario.
- **Trial-class** plans: the PDF may show a **trial caption** and **omit** the full Lion block; **paid** users get the full Lion PDF section when entitled.

## Saving

- Server **save / restore** follows your membership (typically trial = no server save).

## Tips

- **Unlock lines** and **loans** materially change cashflow—review them with your adviser for realism.
- Proceed to **Capital Health** when you want a longer-horizon withdrawal and durability view.

## Disclaimer

Results are **scenario-based** and **not guaranteed**. This is not a recommendation to borrow, invest, or change tax position. Use with a qualified adviser.
